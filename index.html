About Me

I've been coding for over 9 years now, and I'm pretty obsessed with making web apps that are fast, look great, and actually work the way people expect them to. React, TypeScript, and Next.js are my go-to tools for building cool stuff.

I really enjoy leading teams and helping other developers level up their skills. There's something satisfying about taking a messy business requirement and turning it into a clean, intuitive interface that users love.

Right now I'm working at ING as a Senior Frontend Engineer, where I get to build secure banking interfaces and help set the coding standards that keep our codebase clean and maintainable.
